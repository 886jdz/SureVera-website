Copy-ready description for press use

SureVera is a free mobile app that gives diners access to real restaurant health inspection grades from official government sources. Available in 90+ US cities, SureVera helps users make informed dining decisions by surfacing letter grades, inspection details, and violation reports — all sourced directly from city and county health departments. The app is available on iOS and Android.
