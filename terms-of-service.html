2 Description of Service

SureBite is a mobile application available on iOS and Android that provides access to publicly available restaurant health inspection data from official government sources across 90+ cities in the United States.

The Service operates by:

  • Aggregating restaurant health inspection records published by city and county health departments
  • Matching restaurants to their corresponding inspection records using automated name-matching technology
  • Displaying letter grades (A, B, C, or F) and inspection details based on official government data
  • Providing optional push notifications when a restaurant's health inspection grade changes

SureBite does not require user accounts. Certain features may be available through optional paid subscriptions.

3 Data Accuracy and Disclaimers

Please read this section carefully. It describes the nature and limitations of the data displayed in SureBite.

Source of Data. All health inspection data displayed in SureBite is sourced from official city and county health department public records. SureBite does not conduct restaurant inspections, create its own ratings, or generate opinions about any establishment. The grades and inspection details shown in the App reflect findings made by government health inspectors, not assessments or judgments made by SureBite.

Government Grading. Where available, health inspection grades and numeric scores are taken directly from official government records. SureBite uses the following grade scale: A (score of 90 or above), B (80-89), C (70-79), and F (below 70). These grades reflect the scoring systems used by the relevant health departments.

Estimated Scores. In jurisdictions where health departments do not publish numeric scores, SureBite provides estimated comparable scores based on the official inspection findings (such as the number and severity of violations documented). These estimated scores are clearly labeled as estimates within the App. Estimated scores represent SureBite's best effort to translate official inspection findings into a comparable numeric format and should be understood as approximations, not official government scores.

Automated Restaurant Matching. SureBite uses automated fuzzy name-matching technology to match restaurants listed in public databases (such as OpenStreetMap) to their corresponding health inspection records. While we employ confidence thresholds and multiple verification techniques to ensure accuracy, this automated process may occasionally result in:

  • Incorrect matches, where a restaurant is associated with another establishment's inspection record
  • Missing matches, where a restaurant's inspection data exists but is not linked to the correct listing
  • Partial matches, where a restaurant with multiple locations is matched to the wrong branch's inspection

SureBite displays a confidence level for each match. Only matches meeting a minimum confidence threshold are shown to users. However, no automated matching system is perfect, and users should be aware of this limitation.

Data Freshness. The timeliness of inspection data depends entirely on each health department's publishing schedule. There may be a delay of days, weeks, or longer between the date an inspection is conducted and the date it appears in SureBite. Recently inspected restaurants may display outdated grades until the health department publishes updated records and SureBite processes the new data. SureBite updates its data on a regular schedule but cannot guarantee real-time accuracy.

Completeness. SureBite does not claim to display data for every restaurant in every covered city. Coverage depends on the availability of public inspection records, the completeness of restaurant listings in public databases, and the ability of our matching system to link the two. Some restaurants may not appear in the App, and some may appear without inspection data.

No Warranty of Accuracy. SureBite makes no warranty, express or implied, that the data displayed in the App is complete, current, accurate, or error-free. We provide the data on an "as is" basis. Government records themselves may contain errors, and the process of aggregating, parsing, and matching this data may introduce additional inaccuracies.

Not a Substitute for Professional Judgment. The information provided by SureBite is for general informational purposes only. Users should not rely solely on SureBite when making dining decisions, particularly if they have food allergies, compromised immune systems, or other health concerns that make food safety especially important. SureBite is not a substitute for your own judgment or for consulting official health department records directly.

Reporting Errors. If you believe that a restaurant's inspection data, grade, or matching is incorrect, please contact us at support@surebiteapp.com. We investigate all reports and make corrections where appropriate, but we cannot guarantee resolution of every reported issue.
