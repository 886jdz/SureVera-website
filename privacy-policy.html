1. Introduction

SureVera is a product of Arrio Technologies LLC ("we," "us," or "our"). SureVera is a mobile application that displays official government health inspection grades for restaurants across the United States. We are committed to respecting your privacy and being transparent about how we handle data.

This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. SureVera is designed to work without requiring any account creation or personal information.

The short version: SureVera does not require an account, does not collect your name or email, does not track you, and does not sell any data. We only use your location (with your permission) to find nearby restaurants, and your push token (if you enable notifications) to send grade-change alerts.

2. Information We Collect

Information you provide

None required. SureVera does not require account creation. We do not ask for or collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personally identifiable information.

Device information

  • Push notification token: If you enable notifications, your device generates an Expo push notification token. This is an anonymous identifier used solely to deliver grade-change notifications to your device. It is not linked to your name, email, or any personal identity.

Location data

  • GPS coordinates: When you search for nearby restaurants, we request your device's location. This data is sent to our servers to find restaurants in your area. Location access is only used when you explicitly grant permission through your device's operating system.

Usage data

  • Favorites: Restaurants you mark as favorites are stored locally on your device only. We do not have access to your favorites list.
  • Follows: Restaurants you follow for grade-change notifications are stored on our servers, linked to your anonymous push token.

Subscription data

  • In-app subscriptions: Subscription and trial management is handled by RevenueCat. We do not directly collect, process, or store any payment information such as credit card numbers or billing addresses. Please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details on how they handle payment data.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Location: To find restaurants near you. Your GPS coordinates are used to query our restaurant database in real time. Location data is not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Push notification tokens: To send you grade-change notifications for restaurants you follow. Tokens are stored on our servers linked only to the restaurants you follow — not linked to any personal identity.
  • Favorites: Stored locally on your device to let you quickly access restaurants you have saved. We cannot access your favorites.
  • Follows: Stored on our servers, linked to your anonymous push token, so we can notify you when a restaurant's health inspection grade changes.

4. Information We Do NOT Collect

SureVera is built on a principle of minimal data collection. We believe you should be able to check restaurant health grades without sacrificing your privacy.
  • We do not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or any personally identifiable information.
  • We do not store your browsing history or search history on our servers.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs, tracking pixels, advertising identifiers, or any third-party tracking tools.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any user data to third parties.
  • We do not serve advertisements or use ad-related SDKs.

5. Third-Party Services

SureVera relies on the following third-party services to operate. Each of these services may collect their own data according to their respective privacy policies:

  • Expo — Push notification infrastructure. Expo Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat — Subscription and trial management. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Railway — Server hosting for our backend API. Railway Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store / Google Play Store — App distribution platforms. See Apple's and Google's privacy policies.

We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these services to understand how they handle your data.

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local data: Favorites, theme preferences, onboarding status, and your push token are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device (except for the push token, which is sent to our server if you enable notifications).
  • Server-side data: Push notification tokens and restaurant follow records are stored in a PostgreSQL database hosted on Railway's infrastructure in the United States.
  • Encryption in transit: All communication between the SureVera app and our servers uses HTTPS (TLS encryption).
  • Location data: GPS coordinates are used in real time for restaurant searches and are not stored permanently on our servers.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have control over the data SureVera accesses:

  • Disable location access: Revoke location permission in your device's Settings app at any time. The app will still work — you can search by city name instead of using GPS.
  • Disable notifications: Revoke notification permission in your device's Settings app, or unfollow individual restaurants within the app.
  • Delete follows: Unfollow restaurants in the app to remove the associated follow data from our servers.
  • Delete local data: Clear the app's data in your device settings or simply uninstall the app to remove all locally stored data.
  • Delete all server-side data: Use the "Delete My Data" button in the app's Help screen to instantly and permanently delete all server-side data associated with your device, including your device registration, all restaurant follows, notification history, and city requests. This action is immediate and cannot be undone. Your locally saved favorites are not affected.

8. Children's Privacy

SureVera is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children under 13. Since SureVera does not collect personal information and requires no account, there is no way for a child to provide identifiable data. Any server-side data can be permanently deleted at any time using the "Delete My Data" button in the app's Help screen.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal, operational, or regulatory reasons. When we make changes, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this page periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.
