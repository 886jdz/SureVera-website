Why SureVera?

The CDC estimates 48 million Americans — 1 in 6 — get a foodborne illness every year, resulting in 128,000 hospitalizations and 3,000 deaths. The majority of outbreaks are linked to restaurant meals. Health inspection results are public record, but they're buried across hundreds of government websites. SureVera puts them in the palm of your hand.
